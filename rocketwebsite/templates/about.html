About us.

RocketPSA was founded to with the belief that MSPs deserve productive service desk tools, flexible contracts, excellent support, and ownership over their data.

  • 1 Our Mission

    At Rocket PSA, our core purpose is to empower Managed Service Providers with the essential tools and resources they need to thrive in a dynamic and competitive landscape.

  • 2 Our Commitment

    We are committed to simplifying MSPs service operations, streamlining project management, and enhancing overall efficiency, all while keeping our platform accessible and affordable

  • 3 Our Purpose

    At Rocket PSA, our core purpose is to be a catalyst for our customers' success. We strive to be more than just a software provider; we aim to be a strategic partner in the MSPs journey, enabling them to reach new heights of productivity, profitability, and customer satisfaction. By relentlessly pursuing this purpose, we contribute to the growth and prosperity of the MSP community as a whole.
